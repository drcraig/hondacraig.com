Dear Neighbors,

You’re all invited to an

Historic Block Party

On

Saturday, May 8, 2010, 3:00 PM
Rain Delayed: Saturday, June 5, 2010, 3:00 PM

The idea is to have a cookout and potluck, some lawn games for the kids, and to invite you all to bring with you artifacts of the neighborhood from your time here, however long or short; photographs, maps, or simply stories to share. During the party, we’ll have a table out to put your artifacts on display. Afterward, we’ll compile copies of the things you bring as well as pictures from the party into a little pamphlet/newsletter that can be a new artifact for the future.

Interested in coming?

If so, please email Dan Craig so I can try to get some idea of a headcount. Then just bring your families, a small dish or drinks, and whatever artifacts you’d like to share. Feel free to invite others, too!

Interested in helping?

Hopefully it won’t be a complicated affair, but there are several areas where I could definitely use some assistance:

  • Bring a Grill: I have a grill, but it’s definitely not big enough to feed the block. If you have one that’s portable, we can set them all up side-by-side in the driveway and grill together.
  • Bring a Cooler
  • Bring a folding Table or Chairs
  • Help compiling the pamphlet/newsletter

About Us

Dan Craig and his wife Sally Honda moved to the neighborhood this summer from Arlington and Somerville following our marriage. Dan is a software engineer for a nearby aviation electronics company, and Sally teaches classical piano privately out of our home.
